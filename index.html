The DMA aims to promote and maintain good fellowship, enhance opportunity for social development and to propose and promote events for Members of the Deerfield Golf Club.

Upcoming Events

Mark your calendars for these exciting upcoming events!

Member Appreciation Social

April 16, 2025 - 7-9pm

Our first event of the 2025 season has nothing to do with golf. We will be hosting a members social evening to kick off the 2025 season. It is a chance for new and returning members to gather, meet new friends and share stories of great aspirations for the season to come. Hors d'Oeuvres will be provided by the DMA and refreshments will be available to keep you hydrated.

Opening Tournament

May 3, 2025

The Opening Tournament is the kick off to the new golf season. This is a mixed team event where the two lowest net scores are counted on each hole to determine the team score for the hole. Sign up is on an individual basis and the teams are assembled by the tournament convenors.

Les Estey Memorial Tournament

May 31, 2025

The Les Estey Memorial is a mixed 2 person team event (one male, one female) where the lowest net score of the two person team is counted on each hole. You can sign up as a couple or as a single and we will attempt to pair you up with another single on a first come-first served basis. The teams are assembled by the tournament convenors.
